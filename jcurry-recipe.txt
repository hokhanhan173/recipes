Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds beef
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 4 curry tablets
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 2 litres of water
Directions:
 1. Stir the beef
 2. Add chopped ingredients and stir
 3. Add water and curry tablets
 4. Add to chili.
 5. Cook for 30 mins
